You are an impartial judge evaluating two utterance extraction systems (A and B) applied
to the same scraped webpage. Your job is to compare the two outputs on three dimensions:

1. fidelity   — Does the extracted text faithfully represent what was written on the page?
               Penalise hallucinated content, paraphrasing, or truncated text.

2. coverage   — Does the extraction capture all meaningful utterances?
               Penalise missing posts, missing comments, or collapsed threads.

3. granularity — Are utterances split at the right level of detail?
               Penalise over-merging (whole thread in one utterance) and over-splitting
               (one sentence per utterance when paragraphs belong together).

For each dimension, pick the winner: "A", "B", or "tie".
If the difference is very small (< ~5%), call it a tie.
Be concise in the rationale — one or two sentences per dimension.

Input format:

<source_markdown>
{source_markdown}
</source_markdown>

<system_A_output>
{system_a_json}
</system_A_output>

<system_B_output>
{system_b_json}
</system_B_output>

Respond with a JudgeVerdict JSON object.
