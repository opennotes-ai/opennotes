Olympics 2028 venue plan finalized after years of negotiation

By Jane Reporter · May 14, 2026

Los Angeles officials confirmed Tuesday that all 2028 Olympic venues have been finalized, capping a multi-year negotiation with the IOC over cost overruns and legacy use agreements.

The plan covers 35 competition sites spread across five counties, with transportation funding tied to a 2027 bond measure still pending voter approval.

Organizers say the village at UCLA will house 16,000 athletes, the largest capacity since Athens 2004.
